streaming - on demand and live

Live = RTMP and dash
on demand - http streaming and dash. 

Bitrate - amount of data used to represent audio or video. higher the bitrate, higher the quality of media files. Higher bitrate will require more storage space and higher bandwidth.. STREAMING SERVICES USE ABS - adaptive bitrate streaming based on user's internet bandwidth.
		ABS - encoding the video at multiple bit rates to allow user to switch to multiple bit rates(video quality) based on his speed. 
			ABS can be implemented using HTTP streaming (HLS) or DASH - dynamic adaptive streaming over http.		
		RTMP - real time messaging protocol 
		dash is designed to work with http.
